About Us

BestBuilds was created very recently, in September 2020. It is run by one person at the moment, who is aiming to make the computer & component sales industry more accessible and affordable for anybody who would like to enter the PC scene. Whether it be content creation, workstations, gaming, etc. we will have something to suit your needs! You, the customer are our top priority and nothing in the future will change that.
