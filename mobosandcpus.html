A top of the line motherboard, supports 2nd and 3rd generation Ryzen processors. Top of the line capability with onboard with active heatsinks, even more connectivity, and more capable networking functions.

A high quality motherboard supporting 8th and 9th generation Intel processors. Features include great connectivity as well as beautiful RGB lighting.

MSI MPG Z490 GAMING PLUS LGA 1200

Processors

AMD Ryzen 3 3100 Quad-Core, 3.6GHz

$114.99

A great bang for your buck, very affordable processor running at speeds up to 3.6GHz. Requires 65 watts of power, and is extremely capable for its pricepoint. ONLY fits in AM4 socket motherboards.

AMD Ryzen 5 2600 6 Core, 3.4GHz (3.9GHz Boost)

$144.99

A second generation Ryzen processor, but still very high performing today. With the six cores, and boost speeds up to 3.9GHz, and only a 65 watt power requirement it provides excellent value and great performance for gaming. ONLY fits in AM4 socket motherboards.

AMD Ryzen 7 3800XT 8 Core, 3.9GHz (4.7GHz Boost)

$386.99

A very high end Ryzen processor, with 8 cores and a boost speed up to 4.7GHz. Requires 105 watts of power but performs incredibly well. Recommended for high intensity usage such as rendering/media creation, but also fantastic for gaming. ONLY fits in AM4 socket motherboards.

Intel Core i5-8500 6 Core, 3.0GHz (4.1Ghz Boost)

$222.11

An 8th generation Intel 6 core processor with boost speeds up to 4.1GHz, which is a great value for the performance. This processor is extremely fit for gaming, as well as other taxing activities. Requires 65 watts of power. ONLY fits in LGA 1151 socket motherboards.

Intel Core i7-10700K 8 Core, 3.8GHz

$399.99

A top of the line Intel processor, with 8 cores and a boost speed up to 5.1GHz. Insanely powerful and a great processor, but also very expensive. Requires 125 watts of power. ONLY fits in LGA 1200 socket motherboards.
